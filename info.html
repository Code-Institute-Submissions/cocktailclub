Staying in is the new going out!

Here at the Cocktail Club we want to make staying at home the best it can be.
We aim to provide you with a library of cocktail recipes that you can make from the comfort of your own home!
See below for our tips and tricks on making homemade cocktails like a professional

At home cocktail tips and tricks

If you don’t have the infamous cocktail shaker then don’t panic, simply substitute it for an old jar with a screw lid, or a kilner jar.

Some cocktails need to be sieved, if you don’t have any kind of sieve then attach some cling film to the top of your jar with an elastic band and pierce some holes in it!

For the measuring, if you don’t have a shot glass measure, you can use an egg cup or alternatively 3 tablespoon measures.
